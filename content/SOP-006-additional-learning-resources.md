# SOP-006: Additional Learning Resources for CorePLM

## Purpose

This SOP provides training courses, reference materials, and support contacts for CorePLM. It is a companion to the other SOPs in this set and an ongoing reference during the LegacyVault-to-CorePLM transition.

## Scope

All Meridian Medical employees using CorePLM.

---

## Required Training

Complete the following courses before full system access is granted. All courses are available in the Meridian Medical Learning Management System (LMS).

| Course | Course ID | Audience | Time |
|--------|-----------|----------|------|
| CorePLM Fundamentals | PLM-101 | All users | 2 hr |
| Change Management in CorePLM | PLM-102 | All users | 1.5 hr |
| Document Management for Tech Comm | PLM-103 | Technical Communications | 2 hr |
| Part Creation and BOM Management | PLM-104 | Engineering, Manufacturing | 2 hr |
| eManuals Integration | PLM-105 | Technical Communications | 1 hr |
| CorePLM for Quality Teams | PLM-106 | Quality Assurance | 1.5 hr |

## Instructor-Led Training

| Format | Description | How to Register |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Department Onboarding Session | 2-hour live session tailored to your team's workflows | Request through your manager |
| Drop-In Office Hours | Weekly 1-hour open session — Tuesdays 10–11 AM CT, Thursdays 2–3 PM CT | CorePLM calendar on the intranet |
| One-on-One Support | 30-minute session for individual questions | Submit a request via the PLM Support portal |

---

## Support

| Tier | For | Contact |
|------|-----|---------|
| Self-service | General questions | SOPs in this set; CorePLM FAQ on the intranet |
| PLM Support Portal | Access issues, permission requests, general tickets | IT Service Desk → PLM Migration Support queue (SLA: 1 business day) |
| PLM Migration Team | Complex migration questions, workflow configuration, system behavior | plm-migration@meridianmedical.example (SLA: 2 business days) |
| Vendor escalation | Confirmed system defects | Submit through PLM Support portal with "Vendor Escalation" in subject |

For common errors and troubleshooting scenarios, see [SOP-007: Troubleshooting](./SOP-007-troubleshooting.md).
